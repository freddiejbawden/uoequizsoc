Buzzer Evenings
Every week, alternating Wednesdays and Thursdays, we run a free buzzer quizzing session for anyone who wishes to attend. The evening takes the same format as University Challenge, as attendees race against opposing teams to answer questions about a huge number of topics. This is a chance for members to get to know the society members in a smaller group and to try their hand at different styles of questions without the pressure of a serious environment. Afterwards, we usually head over to either the Frankenstein's Quiz (Wednesday) or Southsider's Quiz (Thursday) as a group, to put our warmed up minds to good use!
University Challenge

As well as organising pub quizzes and quizbowl tournaments, EUQS also handles Edinburgh's applications to iconic BBC quiz show University Challenge. We have done well in recent years, making the semifinals in the 16-17 and 17-18 series, and we have a team in the current 18-19 series. If you want to show off your knowledge on TV, you should apply! Going on the show is great fun and very rewarding.

The process starts every year in late October, where we run a huge paper-based quiz for anyone to attend. This year's quiz will be held on Wednesday 24th October 2018, 3-4pm, George Square Lecture Theatre.

The top sixteen quizzers then go to a live buzzer round held a couple of weeks after. This takes the form of some starter questions played as individuals, followed by a mock University Challenge match. A panel of quiz experts (typically the EUQS President, two members of the previous year's University Challenge team, and a representative from EUSA) select the four members of the team. After that, there's an interview with the producers, and after that you're on the show!
